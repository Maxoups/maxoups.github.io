About

This is a little project of mine. I am trying to write a pixel art webcomic loosely based on a game idea I had, and which I may or may not realize one day. Dungeon Crew is a webcomic seriers about a bunch of evildoers doing evil stuff in their evil dungeon.

I also am a game developer and programmer. You can check out my work on itch and github.

This webcomic was in great part made from modified assets packs which were bought or downloaded from itch.io You can check them out here!

I might eventually release my own asset pack, with all of the characters, objects, environments, expressions and creatures that were custom made for this project.

This project was greatly inspired by multiple comics, you can check them out if you want, they're all great:

  • The webcomic 8-bit theater, by Brian Clevinger
  • The webcomic The Order of the Stick, by Rich Burlew
  • The comic book series Dungeon, by Joann Sfar and Lewis Trondheim
  • The graphic novel The Ogre Gods, by Hubert Boulard and Bertrand Gatignol
